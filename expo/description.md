## Problem Statement

Traditional aid distribution suffers from opacity, inefficiency, and a lack of real-time accountability. Donors cannot verify that funds reach intended beneficiaries, and organizations struggle to demonstrate verifiable impact. This erodes trust and reduces the effectiveness of humanitarian efforts.

## Solution

ImpactChain leverages distributed ledger technology to create an end-to-end transparent aid tracking system:

- **On-chain transaction recording** — Every donation, disbursement, and milestone is permanently recorded on the blockchain (Ethereum, Polygon, Celo, StarkNet), creating an immutable audit trail.
- **Real-time impact verification** — Smart contracts automatically verify and trigger milestone-based disbursements, ensuring funds are released only when measurable impact is achieved.
- **Multi-chain & multi-wallet support** — Operates across Ethereum, Polygon, Celo, and StarkNet networks. Users can connect wallets like MetaMask, Argent, and others for seamless interaction.
- **Verifiable credentials** — Beneficiary outcomes (e.g., digital literacy certificates, health outcomes) are recorded as on-chain verifiable credentials.
- **Crypto payments** — Users can send and receive funds in ETH, BTC, and USDC directly through the app using connected wallets.

## Key Features

1. **Impact Dashboard** — Real-time metrics showing total funded amount, beneficiaries reached, on-chain transactions, and verification count.
2. **Project Explorer** — Browse and search active social impact projects across categories (education, health, water, agriculture, governance) with detailed progress tracking.
3. **Transaction Feed** — Live blockchain activity feed showing donations, disbursements, verifications, and milestone completions with full on-chain details.
4. **Wallet Integration** — Multi-chain wallet connection supporting MetaMask, Argent (StarkNet), and other Web3 wallets with token balances across supported networks.
5. **Send & Receive Payments** — Direct crypto payments (ETH, USDC, BTC) through connected wallets for project funding and disbursements.
6. **Project Creation & Management** — Create, publish, and manage social impact projects with milestone-based funding and transparent fund allocation.
7. **Fund Management** — Allocate, track, and disburse project funds with full on-chain transparency.
8. **Block Explorer Integration** — Direct links to on-chain verification for every transaction.

## Alignment with UNICEF Venture Fund Priority Areas

### Area 1: Improving Accountability and Transparency of Existing Services and Payments

ImpactChain directly addresses this priority area by:

- **Reducing operational costs** through automated smart contract disbursements
- **Providing verifiable proof of impact** through on-chain milestone tracking
- **Expanding financial inclusion** for unbanked populations through multi-chain cryptocurrency support
- **Strengthening accountability** through tamper-proof blockchain records
- **Enabling automated, data-driven payments** through blockchain oracles
- **Facilitating due diligence** with transparent, auditable transaction histories

### Area 2: New Financing and Fundraising Models and Local Governance

- **Crowdfunding via crypto** — Projects can receive funding in ETH, BTC, or USDC from global donors
- **Milestone-based disbursements** — Smart contracts release funds only when impact milestones are verified, creating a new accountability-driven funding model
- **Local governance tools** — Community-level decision-making on fund allocation through transparent on-chain voting mechanisms

### Area 3: Incentivizing and Encouraging the Creation of Digital Public Goods (DPGs)

- **Fully open-source** — All code, smart contracts, and protocols are MIT-licensed
- **Reusable infrastructure** — Other organizations can fork and deploy ImpactChain for their own impact tracking needs
- **Sustainable business model** — Platform sustainability through small transaction fees on disbursements, ensuring long-term viability without reliance on grants
