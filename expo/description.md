# ImpactChain — Blockchain for Social Good

## Overview

**ImpactChain** is a mobile-first platform that brings transparency, accountability, and trust to humanitarian aid and social impact funding through blockchain technology. Built as a submission for the **UNICEF Blockchain Ventures 2026 cohort**, ImpactChain addresses the Fund's priority area of **Accountability and Transparency in Services and Payments**.

## Problem Statement

Traditional aid distribution suffers from opacity, inefficiency, and a lack of real-time accountability. Donors cannot verify that funds reach intended beneficiaries, and organizations struggle to demonstrate verifiable impact. This erodes trust and reduces the effectiveness of humanitarian efforts.

## Solution

ImpactChain leverages distributed ledger technology to create an end-to-end transparent aid tracking system:

- **On-chain transaction recording** — Every donation, disbursement, and milestone is permanently recorded on the blockchain (Ethereum, Polygon, Celo), creating an immutable audit trail.
- **Real-time impact verification** — Smart contracts automatically verify and trigger milestone-based disbursements, ensuring funds are released only when measurable impact is achieved.
- **Multi-chain support** — Operates across Ethereum, Polygon, and Celo networks, optimizing for gas costs and regional accessibility.
- **Verifiable credentials** — Beneficiary outcomes (e.g., digital literacy certificates, health outcomes) are recorded as on-chain verifiable credentials.

## Key Features

1. **Impact Dashboard** — Real-time metrics showing total funded amount, beneficiaries reached, on-chain transactions, and verification count.
2. **Project Explorer** — Browse and search active social impact projects across categories (education, health, water, agriculture, governance) with detailed progress tracking.
3. **Transaction Feed** — Live blockchain activity feed showing donations, disbursements, verifications, and milestone completions with full on-chain details.
4. **Wallet & Portfolio** — Multi-chain wallet overview with token balances across supported networks.
5. **Block Explorer Integration** — Direct links to on-chain verification for every transaction.
